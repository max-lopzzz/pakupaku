# Delete Your PakuPaku Account

If you would like to permanently delete your PakuPaku account and all associated data, you can do so directly within the app or by contacting us.

---

## Option 1: Delete from within the app

1. Open PakuPaku and log in.
2. Go to **Settings**.
3. Tap **Delete Account**.
4. Confirm when prompted.

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

---

## Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you are unable to access the app, send a deletion request to:

**m.lopz.montn@gmail.com**

Include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days.

---

## What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

- Your account credentials (email, username, password hash)
- Your nutrition profile and onboarding data (weight, height, measurements, goals)
- All food logs and meal history
- All custom recipes and recipe ingredients
- All body measurement history
- All app preferences

We do not retain any of this data after deletion.

---

## What may be retained

Anonymized or aggregated data that cannot be linked back to you may be retained for analytical purposes. No personally identifiable information is kept after account deletion.
